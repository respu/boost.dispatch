template<typename T, typename Origin = T>
struct boost::dispatch::property_of< T, Origin >

Retrieve the fundamental hierarchy of a Type.

For any type T, returns the hierarchy describing the fundamental properties of any given types. This Fundamental Hierarchy is computed by computing the hierarchy of the innermost embedded scalar type of T.
