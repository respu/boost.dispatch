template<typename T, typename N>
struct boost::dispatch::bag_< T, N >

Homogeneous tuple type hierarchy tag.

Types are classified as bag_ if they represent a compound type that models the FusionSequence concept and which elements are all the same.

By design, bag_ are also classifiable as tuple_.
